Frequently Asked Questions

What is Phantom Power (Standby Loss)?

Even when appliances are physically turned "off", if they remain plugged into a live socket, internal transformers and IC chips draw a continuous current. This is called Phantom Power or Vampire Load. Over 1 year, a household loses an average of ₹3,500 just to this invisibile drain.

How accurate is the financial prediction simulator?

Extremely. We multiply the hardware's recorded standby wattage by 24 hours x 365 days, and multiply that kWh metric against the ₹8.0/kWh standard Indian metric to determine direct fiscal loss per year.

How do I integrate a hardware ESP8266 plug?

Navigate to the `IoT Smart Plugs` dedicated dashboard. Add the localized IP of your ESP32/ESP8266 chip connected to the relay. WattSense's FastAPI backend will then dispatch HTTP ON/OFF requests automatically based on threshold rules.
